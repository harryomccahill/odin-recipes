Lemon Pasta

Delicious Recipe for Homemade Lemon Pasta

Homemade lasagna may have a few steps, but each step is easy – and I assure you it’s worth the time; the perfect Italian meal!

The ingredients in this recipe are all things you know and it’s not difficult at all! All you’ll need for this easy lemon pasta recipe is one pan, one bowl!

  • Lemons
  • parmesan
  • Olive Oil
  • Spaghetti
  • Basil

  1. Squeeze two lemons into a bowl and grate half a block of parmesan.
  2. Add the parmesan and lemon juice into a pan with oil and butter and apply gentle heat.
  3. Boil the pasta and cook to al dente perfection.
  4. Pour the sauce over the pasta once it has been drained and allow to settle.
  5. Rip up some basil leaves over the top and serve.
