Allie's Banana Pancakes

Easy to make and good for your heart

Delicious vegan homeamde pancakes. Super easy to make, all you need is some basic ingredients and you can be enjoying some fluffy healthy panckes of your own

  • One Cup oat Milk
  • Half cup chia seeds
  • Two bananas
  • One Tsp Cinamon
  • Third cup Agave Nectar or equivalent (honey)
  • One cup mixed berries

  1. Mix Chia seeds and Oat Milk for 15 minutes
  2. Separate bowl, whisk bananas and add tbsp water with agave and cinamon
  3. Mix contents of both together
  4. Add desired mixed berries
  5. Coat skillet with tbsp of coconut oil on medium heat
  6. add pancake mix and cook until golden brown
